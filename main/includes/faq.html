FAQ

What is Upframe?

Upframe connects students to established tech entrepreneurs providing them tools and empowering them to become more entrepreneurial.

More than an organization, Upframe is a community made by people who believe in creating an impact in the life of others. People that acknowledge the fact that failing is just another word for growing. Unlike other organisations made by students and attached to universities, we can pride ourselves of being independent and working just like any early stage startup would.

Who are we?

We are a group of darers who live by the following principles:

  • We are dynamic but not over the top.
  • We are flexible without losing our identity.
  • We are ambitious but humble.
  • We are disruptive but grounded.
  • We are fun but not childish.
  • We are young but not irresponsible.
