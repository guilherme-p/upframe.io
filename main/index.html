About

Upframe connects students and recent graduates to the entrepreneurial scene, empowering them to build their own projects and startups.

Startup Playground

What is the Startup Playground?

A pre-accelerator for students, first time entrepreneurs and recent graduates. It works as a club where students are enabled to work on projects, find partners and access workshops and mentors of different backgrounds.

How does it work?

Instead of running for a short and intensive period of time like regular acceleration programs would, Startup Playground consists in 3-hour sessions every Saturday with no end in sight.

When does it start?

Startup Playground will start in Lisbon next 8th of October. The first editions will get massive iterations in order to improve the overall experience for everyone involved.
