How we do it?

We want to help develop and shape the next generation of entrepreneurs. How?

Empower

We provide you personalised support by our dedicated mentors, with years of experience, who will help you reach further in your project

Shape

We want to imprint a problem-solving mindset in our class, by getting them focused on addressing pain relievers and pleasure gainers, instead of developing the "the next big thing"

Connect

Upframe’s network of startups, mentors, incubators, accelerators and like-minded people with whom you can work with, grows every day

Our Programme

Upframe is a pre-acceleration programme that spreads throughout 6 weeks.

Our programme, starting on April 19th and rolling up until May 27th, will include masterclasses by established tech entrepreneurs, our mentors, as well as practical exercises, team building activities and time for you to work on your projects, alongside your colleagues.
