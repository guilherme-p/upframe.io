How we do it

We want to help develop and shape the next generation of entrepreneurs by

Empower

Our mentor expertise, personal and personalised approach and problem-solving mindset will enable you to tackle challenging issues and perhaps, produce a impact.

Shape

Instead of focusing so much in the “startup buzz” or in the development of “the next big thing” our programme is designed to address pain relievers and pleasure gainers and to constantly get you out of your comfort zone enabling you to grow.

Connect

Upframe’s network of startups, mentors, incubators, accelerators and like-minded people with whom you can work with, expands every day.

Our Programme

Upframe is a pre-acceleration programme that spreads throughout 6 different weeks.

The Upframe programme, starting on April 19th and rolling up until May 27th, will include masterclasses by established tech entrepreneurs, our mentors, as well as practical exercises, team building activities and time for you, alongside your colleagues , to work on the projects.
