Our Programme

Upframe is a pre-acceleration programme that spreads throughout 6 different weeks.

The Upframe programme, starting on April 19th and rolling up until May 27th, will include masterclasses by established tech entrepreneurs, our mentors, as well as practical exercises, team building activities and time for you, alongside your colleagues , to work on the projects.
